Here is how to find discord user ID!

It is easy to find your user id on discord.

• Open discord on browser or desktop.

• Go to User Setting Icon in the bottom left corner(next to your name).

• Choose Advanced from the left side bar and turn on the Developer Mode.

• Click on the User Setting Icon again.

• Next to your username and avatar, click on the three horizontal dots, as shown below

• In the popup menu, select Copy ID

• Or, you can simply right-click on your avatar and select ‘Copy ID’.

• Now, you got your user ID and you can paste it on anywhere to use it!
